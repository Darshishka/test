The Northwest Collage Society is an active group of artists committed to advancing the stature of collage as a major art medium. Our organization is entirely volunteer based. Without our volunteers we wouldn't be here. If you are interested in how you can help keep the Northwest Collage Society growing strong, please refer to the job descriptions link below and contact any of our board members for more information.
