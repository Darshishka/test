Current Newsletter - November 2020

Highlights: November program: Cheryl Chudyk's pre-recorded presentation link will be sent out to members on November 17. President's message, 'collaging through a pandemic' fall show awards, Spring show announcement, retreat information, member news, lots of online resources, and links to the international collage community. Volunteer board positions open  - join the community.

We have many volunteer opportunities within the NWCS, and we urge you to participate. Not only will you be helping the NWCS, but you’ll be meeting fellow members and expanding your artistic knowledge. Open volunteer positions are outlined in newsletter. Contact a board member for more information.
