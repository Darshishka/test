Northwest Collage Society Retreat 2021


February 19-21, 2021
Northwest Collage Society Member Retreat

Our 10th annual Northwest Collage Society members retreat at Warm Beach Camp located outside of Stanwood, WA is scheduled! Join us from Friday lunch through Sunday lunch at this beautiful location with a large spacious meeting room. Download sign-up form for more details. If you have further questions contact Wendy at wendy-james@hotmail.com

Retreat Registration form

A $50 deposit is required to hold a reservation. Full payment for the retreat is due by February 9. Due to pandemic concerns, the NWCS Board will decide to proceed with the retreat by Feb. 2nd. If NWCS cancels the retreat, your deposit and fee payment will be returned in full.
