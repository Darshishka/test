March 4 - April 24, 2021
Rock, Paste, Paper, Scissors

The Northwest Collage Society's Spring exhibit, will be held at the Lynn Hanson Gallery in Pioneer Square, Seattle. This show is open to both 2D and 3D collages. There is no theme for this show.
Visit the Lynn Hanson Gallery website

View Prospectus and Entry Details

  • March 4 - Show opens

April 9, 2020 - January 17, 2021
Washington State History Museum, Tacoma, WA

The NWCS is honored to have been invited to participate in the celebration of the 2020 Suffrage Centennial in Washington, organized by the Washington State Historical Society. Collages in the exhibit all relate to the suffrage theme.
